Welcome to GO Videos Channel — the official video streaming app for Gospel Outreach of Olympia. Stay connected to your church community and grow in faith wherever you are.

Whether you're catching up on a message you missed, introducing your kids to Bible-based content, or worshipping through music, GO Videos brings the heart of Gospel Outreach right to your screen.

BROWSE & WATCH
Explore a growing library of content organized by category:
• Sermons — powerful messages from Gospel Outreach's pastoral team
• Children's Videos — engaging, faith-filled content for young hearts
• Music Videos — worship and gospel music to uplift your spirit
• Performances — special events, productions, and church celebrations

LIVE STREAMING
Watch services and special events live as they happen. Never miss a Sunday or a special broadcast — GO Videos keeps you in the room even when you can't be there in person.

PERSONAL PLAYLISTS
Build your own custom playlists from any content in the library. Curate a collection of your favorite sermons, worship songs, or videos for kids — then watch them your way, on your schedule.

FLEXIBLE PLAYBACK
• AutoRun — let the app play through your playlist automatically, perfect for background worship or study sessions
• Shuffle — mix it up and discover content in a fresh order

SEARCH
Find exactly what you're looking for with built-in search. Look up a sermon topic, a speaker, a song, or a series — and get right to the content that matters to you.

AVAILABLE EVERYWHERE
GO Videos Channel works beautifully across your Apple devices:
• Apple TV — enjoy church content on the big screen in your living room
• iPhone — take your faith on the go
• iPad — a rich, comfortable viewing experience wherever you are

A NOTE FROM THE CHURCH
Gospel Outreach of Olympia is a Spirit-filled church committed to sharing the love of Jesus with our community and the world. This app is an extension of that mission — making our content accessible to members, visitors, and anyone seeking faith-based media.

Download GO Videos Channel today and let the Word come alive in your home.
