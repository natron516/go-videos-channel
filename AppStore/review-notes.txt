App Review Notes — GO Videos Channel
Gospel Outreach of Olympia

ABOUT THE APP
GO Videos Channel is a video streaming app for Gospel Outreach of Olympia, a Spirit-filled Christian church located in Olympia, Washington. The app provides access to the church's video library and live streams.

CONTENT
All video content is produced by and for Gospel Outreach of Olympia. Content categories include:
• Sermons — recordings of church services and pastoral messages
• Children's Videos — faith-based video content for children
• Music Videos — worship music and gospel performances
• Performances — special events and church productions

Content is entirely church-produced ministry media. There is no user-generated content. All videos are appropriate for all ages.

LIVE STREAMING
The app supports live streaming of church services via Mux. Live streams are only active during scheduled church services. If no live stream is available during review, please test the on-demand video library which is always accessible.

NO ACCOUNT REQUIRED
The app does not require users to create an account or log in. All browsing and playback is available immediately upon launch.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
• Mux — video delivery and live streaming infrastructure
• iCloud — used for custom playlist sync (Apple-managed; no personal data shared with us)
