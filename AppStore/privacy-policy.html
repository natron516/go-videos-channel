Privacy Policy

GO Videos Channel — Gospel Outreach of Olympia
Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how the GO Videos Channel app handles (or, more accurately, does not handle) your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect no personal data. The GO Videos Channel app does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting tools that transmit personal data, or any third-party tracking. There are no ads in this app.

Custom Playlists & iCloud

If you create custom playlists, they may be stored in iCloud so they sync across your Apple devices. This data is managed entirely by Apple under their privacy framework. We never see or access your iCloud data. For details, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

Video Streaming

Video content in this app is delivered via Mux, a professional video infrastructure service. When you watch a video, your device connects to Mux's servers to stream the content. Mux may collect standard network/delivery data (such as IP address and video performance metrics) as part of their service. We do not control this data. Please review Mux's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. Since we collect no personal data at all, children can use the app safely.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
